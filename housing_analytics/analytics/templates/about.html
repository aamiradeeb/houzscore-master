Housing Analytic Research Group

Founded in 2018, Housing Analytic Research Group has emerged as a key player in the real estate data analytics sector in Malaysia. Our genesis was marked by a collaboration of academicians with a focus on revolutionizing the real estate market through data-driven methodologies. Today, we are recognized for our commitment to assisting homebuyers in making well-informed decisions, backed by robust data analysis.


Our Specialization:
We leverage a distinctive combination of spatial data and advanced analytics to deliver insights into urban dynamics, land-use patterns, and development trends. Our objective is to improve and simplify the home buying process through accurate and comprehensive data analysis.


Our Products and Services:
Comparative Market Analysis (CMA): This tool provides a detailed comparison of properties, considering similar homes and relevant amenities to offer a reliable property valuation.
Housing Analytic Scoring Report: This report evaluates the potential and value of properties based on data-driven insights, presenting a clear and objective analysis.
AI HomeBuyer Advisor: Utilizing AI technology, this tool helps prospective buyers understand their housing loan eligibility and estimate monthly payments, facilitating a more informed financial decision-making process.


In addition to these offerings, we provide a range of services, including market intelligence, location analysis, and sustainability assessments, catering to a diverse clientele in Malaysia and Southeast Asia. Our goal is to empower our clients with data-driven strategies to guide urban development and contribute to a sustainable market.


At Housing Analytic Research Group, we believe in the significant role of data in improving decision-making processes in the real estate sector. Our team, comprised of skilled professionals, is dedicated to leveraging cutting-edge technology and expertise to bring valuable insights to our clients.


We invite you to explore our data-centric approach for a more informed and efficient experience in the real estate market.
